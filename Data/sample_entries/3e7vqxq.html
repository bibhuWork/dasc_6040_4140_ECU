beast v.

[beast n. (9)]

1. to molest a child.

1994I. Welsh \xe2\x80\x98The Shooter\xe2\x80\x99 in Acid House 8: The cunt beasted little Lisa [...] made her go down on him.
2012(con. 1980s) I. Welsh Skagboys 49: Gilbert O\xe2\x80\x99Sullivan wrote that fuckin nonce song aboot beastin bairns.

2. to subject to physical abuse.

2005Guardian 25 Jan. [Internet] Maj Taylor has denied that he ordered the captives to be \xe2\x80\x98beasted\xe2\x80\x99, \xe2\x80\x98thrashed\xe2\x80\x99 and forced to run for three miles.
2018Guardian 2 Jan. CiF [Internet] National service. [L]et the military beast the fuck out of them [i.e. knife-carrying youth].
