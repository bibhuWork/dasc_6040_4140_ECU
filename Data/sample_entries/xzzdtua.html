beast adj.

[survival of schoolboy intensifier beastly, very good, very bad]

1. (W.I.) a general intensifier, both positive and negative.

[1734J. Gay Distressd Wife II viii: You are Beast enough to be fond of the Country, I find, and I am to be lugg\xe2\x80\x99d thither to keep you Company].
